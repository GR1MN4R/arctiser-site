Flagship (In Progress) Comparative Education Chemistry · Biology · Pharmaceutics

The Bachelor Burden

ARCTISER is initiating a comparative study of bachelor-level science programs in Chemistry, Biology, and Pharmaceutics across Norway, Croatia, Serbia, Germany, Czechia, Spain, the United Kingdom, and France.

The project investigates structural intensity in degree design, including:

  • Number of required pass-units (courses/modules)
  • Segmentation of core scientific disciplines (e.g., multi-part course sequences)
  • Laboratory requirements and practical training intensity
  • Assessment density and retake structure (where documented)
  • Timing of specialization within the degree

The initial phase is a pilot comparison (Norway vs Croatia) to validate methodology before scaling to a broader multi-country dataset.

Status: Pilot design & source mapping
Method: Manual curriculum document extraction (pre-digital style)
