Funding & Partners

This page documents ARCTISER’s current funding status and partnership position, and sets out the principles used to protect independence, transparency, and research integrity.

Transparency Independence Disclosure

Current status

ARCTISER currently operates without external funding, institutional sponsorship, or formal partner agreements. All work is conducted independently.

External funding: None
Institutional sponsors: None
Formal partners: None

Funding principles

When ARCTISER receives support in the future, the following principles apply:

  • No influence: funders may not influence research conclusions or editorial decisions.
  • Public disclosure: relevant funding sources will be listed publicly on this page.
  • Documentation: methods, sources, and limitations are stated in published outputs.
  • Independence first: funding with restrictive or compromising conditions is rejected.
  • Non-profit use: resources are used to advance ARCTISER’s purpose and research work.

Partner principles

ARCTISER is open to dialogue and collaboration with universities, research institutions, NGOs, and grant bodies, provided collaboration aligns with ARCTISER’s purpose and preserves independence.

  • Partner contributions and roles are documented.
  • Co-authorship and credit are stated explicitly when applicable.
  • Public communication follows transparency standards.
Contact: research@arctiser.no

Future updates

This page will be updated when funding is received, applications are submitted, or formal partner agreements are established. Updates will list relevant entities and, where appropriate, the scope and timeframe of support.
